Balally Apartment

Two Bedrooms
Dundrum, Dublin, Ireland

Location

The apartment is very close to Dundrum Town Center.

From Dublin Airport you can take Dublin Coach route 750 and get off at stop Dundrum Main Street, then walk about 15 minutes to the apartment.

You can also take tram (Luas Green line) and get off at Balally stop, the apartment is very close to the tram stop.

Dublin Airport Bus

From Dublin Airport you can take Dublin Coach route 750 and get off at stop Dundrum Main Street, then walk about 15 minutes to the apartment.

Tram (Luas)

The tram in Dublin is called Luas. The apartment is very close to the Luas Green line stop Balally.

Parking

There is one parking space available. Parking permit is required, so please send me your car's register plate number, maker (Volkswagen, Toyota, etc.) and car color (balck, white, etc.) before you arrive in order to request parking permit.

Inside The Apartment

The apartment has two bedrooms (double bed and a single bed) with two toilets, a bright living room with large sofa, a dining room and a kitchen. It's perfect for a family stay.

Contact

Please contact me with Airbnb if you have any questions. You can find out the full address and contact details once your booking is confirmed.
